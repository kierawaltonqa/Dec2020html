About us...


QA cinemas was founded in 2021 by Kiera Walton, starting off as a small independent cinema in Dorking, Surrey.

There are now over 5 cinemas located in London, Surrey and York, and there is one opening in Leeds shortly. Each cinema has 2 screens and sofas (instead of chairs). We provide hot food, alcoholic drinks, soft drinks and ice cream - all of which can be brought to customers in their seats.
